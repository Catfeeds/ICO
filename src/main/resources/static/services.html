常见问题/Frequently Asked Questions

最后编辑：2017/7/26 16:16:39

Q1：为什么我的充值一直没到账？

A：充值未到账有三种情况：1. 汇款人姓名及备注码与注册时填写的姓名不符；2. 忘记递交充值订单； 3.充值订单金额与实际汇款金额不符。出现以上三种情况，首先请提供汇款人姓名，如果不是本人汇款，请告知在ICO.TT上注册的用户名；其次，如未提交订单，请提交与汇款信息相匹配的充值信息。

      Q1: I have sent Bitcoins or Ether to the exchange from my wallet, but why does the exchange not show my balance?

       A: This can be due to three reasons: (1) The sender’s name and wallet code does not match with the registration name. (2) The sender forgot to submit the transaction to the exchange. (3) The amount requested to the exchange does not match the actual amount of money in the wallet. If your order has not been submitted, please resubmit your order. If this persists, please contact ICO.TT staff.


Q2：邮箱无法验证（收不到链接，或者链接过期）怎么办？

A：首先可换浏览器尝试，如链接过期或仍旧无法验证，可在后台留言提供验证链接，等待技术人员排查问题。

      Q2: What do I do if my mailbox has not received a verification link or if my verification link expired?

       A: First, you can try changing your browser if the link has expired. If you still cannot be verified, we can provide a verification link in the background message, or you can wait for our technical staff to troubleshoot the problem.


Q3：在ICO.TT上参与投资是否保证成功投资呢？

A：尽管至今为止我们所有锁定的项目全部参投成功，但考虑到部分火爆项目投资时间过短以及不可预料的网络堵塞情况，我们无法承诺确保所有锁定项目必定会参投成功，如果参投失败，我们将会退还所购买的数字货币，请投资者谅解。

      Q3: Does ICO.TT participate in the investment process to ensure a successful investment?

       A: Although all of our locked projects have been successful so far, we cannot ensure that all the locked projects will be successful if the investment time frame is too short or if the network experiences unpredictable congestion. While we try to limit these problems, investors should understand that these situations may occur and we will fully refund your purchase of digital token should an issue arise.


Q4：在ICO.TT上参与的项目什么时候可以领到代币？

A：代币分配并非实时进行，不同代币采用的合约协议不同，上交易所时间也不同，具体以官方时间为准。ICO.TT一般会在项目结束的1-3个工作日内将应得代币数量分配到用户账户，等代币上线后，用户可申请提现，后台会在3-5个工作日内处理完成。提币期间可能会出现区块链堵塞等情况，造成交易延迟，对此ICO.TT不承担币价波动带来的风险损失，望投资者知悉。

      Q4: When will I receive my tokens through ICO.TT?

       A: Tokens are not distributed in real time for several reasons. Different denominations require different contract agreements, differing time zones, etc. ICO.TT tokens are normally distributed to the user's account within 1-3 business days after the ICO is completed. After the project is online, the user may apply for a withdrawal of their tokens, and the order will be processed within 3-5 business days. The block may have already been processed on the blockchain, resulting in an additional delay for the transaction. ICO.TT will not be held responsible for currency fluctuations caused by such delays.


Q5：申请人民币及数字货币提现一般多久可以到账？

A：1-3个工作日，数字货币提现可能会出现卡区块、Gas不足等情况，如遇到此类问题请留言，后台工作人员会优先处理。

      Q5: How long does it take to exchange RMB for digital currency?

       A: It usually takes 1-3 business days. In cases where the user receives a notice that the transfer of digital currencies are being delayed (card block, gas shortages, etc), our staff will prioritize the troubleshooting of the issue.


Q6：数字货币充值一直不到账怎么办？

A：如区块链已确认，可以点击个人信息页面>>数字货币充值标签>>下拉到页面底部>>查看充值状态。

      Q6: I received less digital currency than I paid for! What do I do?

       A: If the transaction has been confirmed on the blockchain, you should: Click on the personal information page → Digital Money Recharge Label → Scroll down to the bottom of the page →View your recharge status.


Q7：收到项目代币后如何交易？

A：此类问题请关注项目官网，不同项目代币的存放平台不同，上线的交易所也不同，请关注官方信息。

      Q7: How do I trade after receiving the project tokens?

       A:To keep up with the different project tokens, please refer to the official website of each project. Different projects often utilize different token storage platforms as well as different exchange platforms. Therefore, investors must be vigilant and keep up with information presented by official sources.
